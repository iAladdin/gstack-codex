# Office Hours

1. Read `../references/principles.md`.
2. Read `../references/planning.md`.

## Goal

Turn a feature-shaped request into a pain-shaped plan.

## Output

- user pain in plain language
- hidden job-to-be-done
- assumptions worth challenging
- 2-3 approaches
- the narrowest high-learning wedge
- what to validate next

Push back on framing when the user is solving the wrong problem.
