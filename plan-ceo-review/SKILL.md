# Plan CEO Review

1. Read `../references/principles.md`.
2. Read `../references/planning.md`.

## Focus

- Is the plan too small, too broad, or exactly right?
- What is the real wedge?
- What should not be built yet?
- Which decision most changes the upside?

Pick and explain one mode: `Expand`, `Selective Expansion`, `Hold Scope`, or `Reduce`.
